What we do

Extending the life of British produce

Ice & Jar was founded to solve a quiet crisis in the British food supply chain: vast quantities of high-quality, nutritious produce are lost every year — cosmetically imperfect, surplus to supermarket specification, or downgraded on the basis of assumptions that have never been tested.

We work with regenerative and organic British farms to transform that produce into minimally processed, naturally preserved ingredients — through freezing, fermenting, pickling and curing. No synthetic additives. Nothing unnecessary. Just proper food, handled properly.

Our products supply institutional catering markets: schools, NHS hospitals and workplace canteens — getting genuinely good British food onto the plates of the people who need it most.

Three things
we believe in

Nature-friendly farming

We partner with regenerative and organic farms that put soil health, biodiversity and long-term stewardship first. How the food is grown matters as much as how it is made.

Minimal processing

We use only natural methods — freezing, fermenting, pickling, curing — to extend shelf life. No synthetic additives, no flavour masking. The food tastes like itself.

Institutional impact

We focus on the markets that matter most: schools, hospitals and NHS catering — where better ingredients make the biggest difference to the most people.
